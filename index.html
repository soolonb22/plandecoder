Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Who is this for?
Any NDIS participant with an upcoming plan review or assessment. Also carers, support coordinators, and plan managers. It is especially useful if your plan includes community participation or capacity building supports.
Is the free version really free?
Yes. No sign-up. No credit card. No catch. The free preview covers your first 3 life areas and runs entirely in your browser. Full access unlocks all 12 areas and your complete summary.
What if I have just had my plan renewed?
Policy changes can still affect you mid-plan. Using this tool helps you understand your current support needs. It also helps you prepare for any review that comes up.
What does preparation support include?
An experienced preparation consultant reviews your summary. They point out gaps. They suggest clearer ways to describe your needs. They give you a personal action plan. This is a preparation and coaching service. It is not legal, medical, or registered disability advocacy advice.
Is this an official NDIS or NDIA service?
No. Plan Decoder is fully independent. We are not affiliated with the NDIA or the Australian Government. Our tools help you understand and prepare for the official process. They do not replace it. All NDIS decisions are made by the NDIA.
Is my information private?
Yes. The prep tool stores everything on your device only. Nothing you enter is sent to any server. If you make a purchase, payment is handled securely by Stripe. Plan Decoder does not store your card details.
